For the Kids!

THON is the world's largest student-run philanthropy. To this date, THON has raised $180 million in our fight against pediatric cancer.

Tech stack: Django (Python 3.5), HTML, CSS. CI/CD via Travis CI.

I have had the privilege of volunteering as a technology captain for two years in college. My first year was spent coding for the THON Weekend Dashboard as a Dash developer, and my second year was spent as the Special Projects Lead Developer, where my team created the Quizzes platform. Developing our applications in Django, I had the unique experience of having 50,000 users use features I and my team developed, all the while supporting an amazing cause.


The links to our products will be provided, but you will be unable to view further than the home view since you need Penn State login credentials.


Some cool things:

  • During my time volunteering (2015-2019), we raised $43,939,563!
  • We had 23 members in the committee, supporting 3 applications that service 50,000 users.
  • Our sponsors include Dell, who provisioned laptops and servers for us, and now Amazon AWS! (Sadly, the year after I left.)
  • Our fundraising efforts culminates to a 46-hour dance marathon. Captains have two 4-hour sleep shifts, which meant that we sometimes had to push hot fixes while being awake for 30+ hours.
