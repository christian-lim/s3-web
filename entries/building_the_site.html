Building the Site

From development to deployment.

Summary

This site utilizes AWS S3's static webhosting capabilities. The setup was simple, well documented, and the maintenance is dirt cheap.

I wanted to create a site back in senior year. I didn't realize the scope of AWS back then, so I had a pretty naive approach of spinning up EC2 instances. I played around a bit, but ended up spending around $20 just for keeping my instance running. Enter my discovery of static web hosting. This service costs 50 cents a month! So I just slapped in my html/css from my original Django project, registered a domain and some necessary routing records in Route 53, and voila!
